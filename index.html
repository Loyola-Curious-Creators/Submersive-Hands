About us

At Submersive Hands, our core mission revolves around environmental stewardship, particularly in addressing the pressing issue of ocean pollution. We specialize in the development of advanced submarine technologies meticulously designed to effectively locate and retrieve stranded ghost nets and marine debris. Our endeavors are underpinned by rigorous research, strategic partnerships with leading environmental organizations and research institutions, and a commitment to technological innovation. Through our dedication to excellence and sustainability, we aim to make a meaningful impact on the health and preservation of our oceans.

About our first model

  • The workings of the arm

    Each arm is meticulously controlled by a trio of motors, meticulously calibrated to manage specific functions such as lateral articulation, extension/retraction, and claw closure.

  • The cicuit of the submarine

    The Quarky board serves as the central control unit, orchestrating the synchronized operation of multiple motors responsible for arm articulation and propulsion.

  • Operation

    Remote operation via an intuitive mobile application or dedicated controller interface ensures ease of use and seamless control for operators.
